My favorite interests :

1) Video Games :

Since my youngest childhood, i am playing and enjoying video-Games. It is one my favorite interests. I’ve played a lot of video games in my life. I started video games at the age of 6 or 7 years old with old video game consoles, like PlayStation 1. I like video games because we can embody any character we want. We can be a magical creature or even an animal. Also, stories in video games are so interesting. There are a lot of video-game categories but i would say that I like adventure, point & click, and action video-games. My favorite game of all time is "League Of Legends". It is a strategical video game. The rules are simple : there are two teams and they are both fighting to get access to their opponent’s base, called "nexus". Destroying ennemy’s base leads to victory and it requires years of trainings to be good at this game, because it is so technical. This video-game is amazing because every party is never the same, and it’s always stimulating to play.
