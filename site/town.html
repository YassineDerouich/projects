An introduction to my town

I am currently living in Malakoff, so i will talk a bit about this city !

Malakoff is a small town of 30 000 inhabitants, located in the region of "Ile de France". The city is close to Paris, less than 20minutes from it ! "Malakoff" is taking his name from a tower in the 19th century that was taken by France against the Prussians, during the Crimée War.

What can we do in Malakoff?

There is almost everything you need in Malakoff! You have shops, schools, various clubs (sports, chess, writing,etc…),three medical centers, restaurants, and more ! The city is quite calm ! It is perfect for people that like peace and calm ! If you don’t like when it’s too much calm, you’re close to Paris!

Benefits of living in Malakoff:

  • -The city is calm
  • -There is almost everything you need
  • -People are kind
  • -The city is close to Paris

Disadvantages of living in Malakoff :

  • -Not that much activities to do for fun
  • -Almost no tourism in Malakoff, not that much things to visit
  • -The city itself is not that much beautiful
  • -The city itself is not a land of opportunities and activities
