An introduction to the IUT of Velizy and what are my plans for the future after studying here !

Here's a short video about the IUT :

The University Institute of Technology of Vélizy, located in the city of Vélizy-Villacoublay is a public education institute that trains people to computer science, in different specializations. The IUT trains people after the Baccalauréat by proposing them 2 and 3 years degrees, respectively named University Degree of Technology and University Bachelor of Technology. The IUT can trains you into multiple specializations inside computer science:

  • -Computer Science (programming)
  • -Network Computing & Telecoms
  • -Multimedia Computer Jobs
  • -Electronical and industrial Computer Science
  • -Cybersecurity

Why did i chose to study here?

I chose the IUT of Velizy because it is known for its good quality of learning. Most of the teachers of the IUT are also scientific researchers that are specialized in their domain, which gives to students a high quality of learning. I chose the University Bachelor of Technology in "Network Computing & Telecoms" because during my last year of high school i loved so much network computing courses that i knew this degree would be the perfect one for me.

What I hope doing after my B.U.T Network Computing & Telecoms?

My plan for the future is to become a Network Engineer or a Network Administrator, depending if I pursue or not my studies.
